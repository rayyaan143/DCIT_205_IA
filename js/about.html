GREETINGS

Explore the Field of Computer Science to Discover the Power of Technology

"Say hello to a digital environment where knowledge and creativity collide and the seemingly endless potential of technology is unlocked. Your portal to the exciting field of computer science is Department of Computer Science, where we set out to discover, comprehend, and honor the marvels of the digital era. The Computer Science Department is a vibrant and cooperative space where using programing to explore and understand the world around us, at the center of our academic community. We have a long history of success and are dedicated to promoting an environment that values imagination, critical thinking, and practical experience.The ability to program computers is at the core of computer science, and companies hiring computer scientists look for skilled programmers who understand current methods and tools and can apply good engineering principles to the creation, building, and upkeep of computer programs.Our graduates are prepared for the computing industry by the variety of courses offered in the department and the skills they acquire. These courses enable students to work on  projects as a team,  or other setting where they can contribute specialized skills. Additionally, by exposing students to current computer science research topics and both established and newly developed technologies and methodologies.





Distance Learning

Witness a UG classroom from anywhere you are via online learning
Through a variety of online delivery options, you can benefit from
real-time lectures and personal engagement with professors while
tailoring your degree to your schedule. Our online classroom maintains
the same level of academic rigor while offering students with additional
learning opportunities.
