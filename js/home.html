EMBARK ON A JOURNEY OF EXCELLENCE IN COMPUTER SCIENCE

WHY STUDY COMPUTER SCIENCE?

Scholars in computer science ask questions like, "Can we build computers that think like humans?" How might computer games with growing characters be improved?
What does the future of augmented reality look like?Computer science is a theoretical and practical problem-solving discipline that combines creativity, mathematics,
logic, and communication. A solid understanding of computer science principles can help you prepare for a lengthy career of problem solving and system design without
the need for ongoing training.Throughout your study, you will be actively solving issues and developing new programs in class and tutorial laboratories. Students
interested in acquiring expertise in computer science research might look for volunteer or paid opportunities to work in research laboratories.
